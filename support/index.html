Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device only. We never collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your data never leaves your device.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings → Delete All Data. You can also delete individual entries from the History tab in Statistics.

Can I backup my data?

Currently, data is stored locally and not included in iCloud backups for privacy. Export feature coming soon.

How does the Wellness Score work?

The Wellness Score (0-100) is calculated based on: frequency balance, variety in activities, goal adherence, and mood patterns.

What do the streak numbers mean?

"Current" shows consecutive days with logged activity. "Best" shows your longest streak ever achieved.

How do I switch between dark and light mode?

Go to Settings and toggle the Dark Mode switch.
