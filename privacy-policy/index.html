Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

🔒 Your privacy is our priority. LOG31 is designed with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device - we never collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Data Collection

LOG31 does not collect any personal data. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Store data on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties

2. Local Storage

All data you enter in LOG31 is stored locally on your device only:

  • Session logs and history
  • Personal goals
  • Statistics and patterns
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features:

  • Device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • iOS sandboxing and encryption
  • No cloud backup of sensitive data

4. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual entries at any time
  • Clear all data from Settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data

5. Third-Party Services

LOG31 does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions entirely offline.

6. Children's Privacy

LOG31 is intended for users 17 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
